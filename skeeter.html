Skeeter, Go Faster!

"Skeeter, Go Faster!" is an endless runner mobile game where the player controls a car on an oval race track. The car must be used to mow down hordes of zombies to prevent said zombies from entering the base that is situated in the centre of the race track.

While mowing down the zombies, the player must be cautious not to run down survivors that are crossing the race track to bring in supplies to the camp. Furthermore, obstacles such as caravans, oil spills, and ramps will be spawned on later waves that the player must deal with. Some of them damage the car, while others can be used for jumping, spinning etc.

The game was created in Unity3D with the 3D assets created in Blender.
