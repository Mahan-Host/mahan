Steppin' Stones

Steppin' Stones is a portable playground that combines elements from the classic childhood games "catch" and "hot lava".

The playground consists of portable pressure pads that can be combined in endless ways to facilitate play. The course can be put up in most places and vary based on placement and number of pads. It is then up to the user to determine if the course is used for a game with rules ala "catch", or just open play ala "hot lava" or something else entirely.

The prototype consist of foam pads that detect pressure via an Arduino microcontroller. When the sensor detects pressure, it can then be used to create vibration, sounds, light. etc. in the pads to invoke different reactions in the player and invoke play.
